Background

The Japanese engineer, Taiichi Ohno identified that companies should spend more time on work that is useful, productive and valuable to the Customer, and minimise activity that is meaningless and wasteful.

Ohno described Muda (Waste) in 7 categories (now 8), and how companies should identify and eliminate Waste to prosper.
